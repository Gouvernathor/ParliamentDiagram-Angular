Arch-shaped parliament diagram generator

To add a party to be displayed, click the button to "Add a party manually", as many times as there are parties to display. Then, fill the number of seats, the display color, and the party name for each.

After filling in the parties, click "Make my diagram", and it will be generated and displayed. A button will enable you to download it.
You can then freely use the diagram, but to use it in Wikipedia, you need to upload it to Wikimedia Commons.

If the layout of the seats in the diagram seems strange, or if you want to customize it, you can try other options in the "Advanced parameters" section.

Please submit bug reports and feature requests at the project's issue tracker.
