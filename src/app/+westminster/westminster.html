Westminster-style parliament diagram generator

This is a tool to generate diagrams of a Westminster-style parliament, composed of a left bench, a right bench, an optional cross-bench group, and a speaker.

Add parties to be displayed, filling (for each party) the area it belongs to, its number of seats, and the color used to display it.

After filling the parties, click "Make my diagram", and it will be generated and displayed. A button will enable you to download it.
You can then freely use the diagram, but to use it in Wikipedia, you need to upload it to Wikimedia Commons.

Please submit bug reports and feature requests at the project's issue tracker.
